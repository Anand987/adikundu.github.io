Campskill

I worked on the development of the visual identity of CSE Association, the student-run association of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, SRM IST.

Worked on the design and media of the events organized by CSEA in the academic year 2017-2018.

july 2017

GOAL

The existing logotype lacked a solid appeal and was incoherent with the rest of the design system. The rectangular low-poly logo with the circular bird did not fit alongside rest of the brand.

The previous logo for Campskil.

The company was a fledgling startup and needed a visual identity that exhibits the youthfulness but also the ambition of the founders. The warm and youthful colours in the existing logo however, were a strong factor embedded within it.

During initial interviews with the stakeholders within the company, it became known that they would like Campskil to represent youth and freedom. These two emotions formulated the core ethos of the company.

Logomark of the revamped brand

During this conceptualisation phase, several interviews and surveys were undertaken to help forge and validate the core elements of the redesign.

Early concepts and user interviews about key emotions evoked by the brand elements.

People were asked to describe the emotions or memories that were evoked by the ‘target words’ of the identity in development. These were ‘bird’, ‘wings’ and ‘origami’.

Over half the people interviewed linked the target words to freedom and independece. Also, 80% of them said that origami reminds them about childhood. This validation of user empathy was taken forward into early sketches and the formulation of the further identity.

VISION

The redesign of the visual identity of Campskil started with one word, Freedom. The freedom to explore. The freedom to learn. The freedom to innovate. At it’s heart, Campskil is a company that enables individuals to develop skills that are not bound by textbooks or curriculum.

Logomark of the revamped brand

"The origami shaped “Rising Bird” symbolizes a free mind that is taking off to explore new opportunities that await in the journey of one’s life. The origami style of the logo is reminiscent to our childhood days where by using a 6”x 6” origami sheet we were asked to let our creativity run wild and try to create all sorts of shapes. The thrill of exploring new paths is what motivates this logo.

The period in the word gives it a sense of finality. It signifies that the days of torment are over. Now one can truly fly and explore themselves together with Campskil. It also hints that once you’re with us, there is no need to search further.”

Quoted from the Campskil Brand Guidelines.

The various versions of the logomark and its inhabiting elements were designed. These are designed to be availale for use in a variety of cases.
